**Description:**
After an incident, the real question is never "what happened" — it's "why was that allowed to happen?" Answering that requires proof of the authorization state at the exact moment of the event. Most systems can only offer logs and inference. Few can offer cryptographic proof.

Genesis Mesh produces a signed trust evidence record for every significant authorization decision — agreements, attestations, boundary decisions, consensus outcomes, data-usage disclosures — each signed with the operator's Ed25519 key and verifiable offline. The audit trail isn't a report assembled after the fact. It's the protocol's direct output, useful for post-incident review, compliance, and day-to-day operational confidence across sovereign boundaries.
